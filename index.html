How Scores Are Calculated ▶

The GFI score is calculated in three steps: indicator normalization, category aggregation, and weighted final score.

1. Indicator Normalization

Each policy slider (s) is normalized to a value (n) from 0 to 1.

n_i = s_i / 10

2. Category Score

Scores for each of the five categories (C) are calculated by averaging the normalized scores of their constituent policies. All policies within a category are weighted equally.

C_k = (Σ n_i / count(n_i)) * 100

3. Overall GFI Score & Weights

The final GFI is a weighted average of the five category scores.

GFI = Σ (C_k * W_k)
